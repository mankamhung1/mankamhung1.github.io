MAN Kam Hung 

He believes that inclusiveness, respect and understandings are the key elements
for a more peaceful human society.
After a year of education of Applied Physics in Hong Kong Polytechnic University,
he went to the School of Creative Media in City University of Hong Kong for
further study and earned a B.A. in Creative Media.

After graduation, he worked as a supervisor for 2 years in a Japanese retail support
company AJIS which provides stock-taking and mystery shopping services.
Overwhelmed by long working hours and night shifts, he quitted the job and joined
his friends to form a group called "GOX" (the word has no specific meaning,
but they like how it sounds and its pronounciation is similar to the
Cantonese word "覺醒" - Enlighten).
GOX's members are going to Beijing in late October 2017 for an art-day
in a lifestyle hotel, CHAO. The project concerning the dangerous and old
builings in China and the potential of upcycling the dismantled concrete would
be shown in the exhibition.
MAN is responsible and facing difficulties for the software (written in C++ openframeworks)
which would be used for the content display of the project. The content would include
video interviews of the people around the investigation area, images
and some images movement with certain degree of randomness.

MAN plays darts and he is a cat and music lover.
